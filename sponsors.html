Becoming a Sponsor

We rely on the financial support of various corporations and organizations to ensure the best possible support for meeting attendees. If you are willing to help in this regard, please e-mail Mark Benvenuto at benvenma@udmercy.edu. Your logo will be featured on the front-page of our website as well as being displayed prominently throughout the meeting. Thank you.
