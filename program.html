Technical Program

Get Involved! Suggest a Technical Session/Symposium Topic

This form will accumulate suggestions for the technical session/symposium topics for the 2015 Joint Great Lakes Central Regional Meeting of the American Chemical Society to be held May 27 - May 30 in Grand Rapids. The overall theme of the meeting is “Chemistry - A Grand Enterprise”, and will focus on three main areas food, health, and the environment.
